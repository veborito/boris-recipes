Protein Pancakes

Description

High protein breakfast, fast and easy, with 36 grams of protein.
Estimated time to prepare ~ 20 minutes. The measurements are included below.
If you're not a good cook don't worry, you'll work your way through this!
Remember be patient and don't put your hand in the hoven! (please)

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Sweetener
  • Plain Greek yogurt
  • Protein powder
  • Baking powder

Steps

  1. In a bowl whisk the eggs, vanilla, stevia, and yogurt
  2. Add the protein powder and whisk until completely smooth and blended
  3. Whisk in the baking powder
  4. Cook the pancakes until they bubble on top
